HOL01 Use REX-Ray & ScaleIO with Docker, Mesos, and Kubernetes

Containers are rapidly being adopted by companies large and small. Yet, how do you know which container platform is right for you? Use REX-Ray, an open source storage orchestration engine from {code} by Dell EMC, along with ScaleIO and learn how to run stateful and persistent applications on the most popular cloud native platforms: Docker, Mesos and Kubernetes.
